Our Approach

Rigorous research,
disciplined capital

4Capital was founded in 2024 by four students from complementary disciplines — law, business, chemical engineering, and business engineering. This cross-disciplinary edge lets us approach every investment the way a lawyer constructs an argument and an engineer solves a system.

In our first year we focused entirely on fundamental research — building the analytical foundation before deploying capital. In 2025 we are committing our initial €2,000 to a concentrated portfolio of high-conviction positions, guided by evidence, not noise.
